Who We Are

StratusEdge is a minority-owned and woman-owned consulting firm dedicated to providing high-impact talent solutions tailored to the evolving needs of today’s businesses. We specialize in executive and non-executive recruitment across a broad range of industries, combining strategic sourcing expertise with deep market insight. Our hybrid model—rooted in U.S.-based dependability and enhanced by offshore scalability—enables us to deliver agile, cost-effective staffing solutions that empower organizations to build exceptional teams and drive long-term success.
